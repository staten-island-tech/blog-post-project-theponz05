Rich Snob Food Blog My Experience with Expensive Food Food
A Rich Snob's Favorite Pizza Hello, I am a rich person snob. I only eat the highest quality of food, and I will not eat anything a dirty commoner would eat. Pizza is some of my favorite food, but I would never, and I mean never, eat a pizza that costs under 500 bucks. My favorite pizza is lined with gold flakes. Once, a horrifying occurrence happened to me. I was flying on my private jet to an expensive pizza place halfway across the country. Once I made it there, I ordered my favorite pizza, the $5,000 pizza, but they said they were out! Horrifying! I had to order normal people pizza for $50 and as I bit into it, I threw up in my mouth, and nearly threw up all over the table. From then, I vowed to never eat food that costs under 500 bucks.
