A Rich Snob's Favorite Pizza

Hello, I am a rich person snob. I only eat the highest quality of food, and I will not eat anything a dirty commoner would eat. Pizza is some of my favorite food, but I would never, and I mean never, eat a pizza that costs under 500 bucks. My favorite pizza is lined with gold flakes. Once, a horrifying occurrence happened to me. I was flying on my private jet to an expensive pizza place halfway across the country. Once I made it there, I ordered my favorite pizza, the $2,000 pizza, but they said they were out! Horrifying! I had to order normal people pizza for $50 and as I bit into it, I threw up in my mouth, and nearly threw up all over the table. From then, I vowed to never eat food that costs under 500 bucks. A Rich Snob's Favorite Steak

I said to him “How can you eat that stuff?”, he responded saying that he thought I liked steak, and I told him that the only way it would taste good is with gold. He told me to just try it and as soon as I put it in my mouth, I fell over from how bad it tasted. A Rich Snob's Favorite Hamburger

Rich snob here for the final time. Let me tell you about hamburgers. They are bad, unless- you guessed it- they have gold flakes on them. Regular burgers look putrid, unless they have gold on them. My story about hamburgers is when I went to a new place with expensive hamburgers, I ordered a hamburger with golden flakes. When I got my burger there were only a couple golden flakes, and when I bit into it I could tell it was low quality gold. “WHAT IS THIS STUFF?” I screamed. The server told me that high quality gold was too expensive to put on a burger. I was so upset that I threw the burger at the server and screamed “THIS PLACE STINKS!” and ran out the door.
